Safety & Guidelines – Commercial LPG Cylinder (19 KG)

Haryana Indian Gas safety ko sabse upar rakhta hai. 19 KG Commercial LPG Cylinders ka sahi use aapke business aur staff dono ke liye bahut zaroori hai. Neeche kuch important safety guidelines diye gaye hain jo har commercial kitchen ko follow karne chahiye.

  • Cylinder hamesha seedhi (upright) position mein rakhein.
  • Cylinder ko hamesha achhi ventilation wali jagah par use karein.
  • Gas stove aur regulator ko regularly check karte rahein.
  • Leakage check karne ke liye kabhi bhi aag ya matchstick ka use na karein.
  • Gas smell aane par turant cylinder band karein aur hawa ka flow badha dein.
  • Commercial kitchen mein fire extinguisher hona anivarya hai.
  • Sirf trained staff ko hi LPG cylinder handle karne dein.
  • Cylinder ko heat source se door rakhein.

In safety rules ka palan karna na sirf kanooni zaroorat hai balki aapke business ki suraksha ke liye bhi bahut mahatvapurn hai. Haryana Indian Gas aapko safe aur reliable LPG supply dene ke liye committed hai.
